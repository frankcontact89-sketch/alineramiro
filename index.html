Biography

About Aline Ramiro

Aline Ramiro is a Christian author and Bible teacher devoted to helping people understand Scripture, discover their identity in Christ and grow in their relationship with God.

Her work brings together biblical education, prayer, discipleship and leadership development through initiatives connected with Prayer & Fire Global Movement, Movement Church and ABA Academy.

She is also the author of Voz Interior: Os Segredos de Uma Identidade Ativada, a book that explores identity, purpose and spiritual transformation.
