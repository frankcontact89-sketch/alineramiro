About Aline Ramiro

Aline Ramiro is a Christian author and Bible teacher devoted to helping people understand Scripture, discover their identity in Christ and grow in their relationship with God.

Her ministry work is connected with Prayer & Fire Global Movement, Movement Church and ABA Academy, initiatives centered on prayer, discipleship, biblical education and Christian service.

She is the author of Voz Interior: Os Segredos de Uma Identidade Ativada, a book about identity, purpose and spiritual transformation.
