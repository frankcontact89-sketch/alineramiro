Biography

About Aline Ramiro

Aline Ramiro is a Bible teacher, Christian author and ministry leader dedicated to helping people grow in their knowledge of Scripture, understand their identity in Christ and develop a deeper relationship with God.

Her ministry is connected with Prayer & Fire Global Movement, Movement Church and ABA Academy, initiatives focused on prayer, biblical education, discipleship, leadership and global Christian service.

Through biblical teaching and Christian literature, Aline shares messages about identity, purpose, spiritual maturity, intimacy with God and faithful service to Jesus Christ.
